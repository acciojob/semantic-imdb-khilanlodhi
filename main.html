Summary

"The Shawshank Redemption" follows the story of Andy Dufresne, a banker who is wrongly imprisoned for the murder of his wife. Inside Shawshank prison, he forms a lasting friendship with fellow inmate Red and finds hope, redemption, and freedom through persistence and quiet rebellion.
